Terms and Privacy

Clear, human, and simple.

This page explains what I need to organize your booking and how your shared details are handled.

What I collect

Only the practical information needed to plan your shoot: names, wedding date, city/location, preferred package, and your contact in chat (Instagram, Telegram, or WhatsApp).

How I use it

I use your details only to communicate, confirm booking terms, plan timeline, and deliver your gallery. I do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising.

Photo storage and backup

Delivered galleries are backed up for up to 1 year after delivery. After that period, archive availability is not guaranteed.

Deletion request

You can request deletion of your contact and planning details at any time by writing in the same chat channel used for booking.

Booking terms (short)

Booking is confirmed with 50% prepayment. Remaining 50% is paid on the wedding day. Delivery timeline for edited photos is 4 weeks unless agreed otherwise in writing.
